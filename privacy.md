# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 11, 2026**

Lumino Studio Pty Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (https://lumino.au/) and use our mobile applications.

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services:
* **Voluntary Information:** If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and any information you choose to provide.
* **App Data (Future):** Our upcoming applications may collect non-identifiable usage data to improve user experience. Specific data collection practices will be disclosed within each app.

## 2. Use of Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
* Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
* Comply with legal obligations (e.g., Apple Developer Program requirements).
* Improve our website and software products.

## 3. Australian Privacy Principles (APP) Compliance
As an Australian entity, we comply with the *Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)*. 
* **Data Storage:** Your information is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized personnel.
* **Access and Correction:** You have the right to access or correct any personal information we hold about you by contacting us at the email below.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our upcoming educational applications are designed with children's privacy in mind. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent, in compliance with the Privacy Act and App Store Guidelines.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. We may use trusted third-party service providers (such as GitHub Pages for hosting) that adhere to strict privacy standards.

## 6. Automated Decision Making
*Note: In accordance with the December 2026 Australian Privacy updates, we do not currently use automated decision-making processes that significantly affect individuals.*
