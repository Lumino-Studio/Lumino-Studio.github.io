Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Lumino Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates as a professional business entity in Australia. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services:

  • Voluntary Information: If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and the contents of your message to respond to your inquiry.
  • App Data: Our educational applications are designed to function without collecting personally identifiable information (PII). Any progress data is stored locally on your device.

2. Children’s Privacy (App Store & COPPA)

Our Priority: Our educational applications are designed for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personal information from children. No account registration is required to use our learning modules.

3. Use of Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Respond to support inquiries and technical questions.
  • Comply with legal obligations and Apple Developer Program requirements.
  • Monitor website traffic via GitHub Pages to improve our site’s performance.

4. Australian Privacy Principles (APP) Compliance

As an Australian entity, we comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth):

  • Data Security: We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, or unauthorized access.
  • Access & Correction: You have the right to request access to any personal information we hold about you or request its correction.
  • Automated Decision Making: In accordance with 2026 updates, we confirm that we do not use automated processes that significantly impact individuals.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We use trusted service providers like GitHub Pages for hosting, which adhere to industry-standard security and privacy protocols.
