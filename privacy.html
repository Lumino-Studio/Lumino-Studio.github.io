Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

Lumino Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our applications and services.

1. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Some of our educational applications are designed for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from children. There is no account registration required to use our learning modules.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data such as names, addresses, or email addresses. Our apps may store progress data (such as completed lessons) locally on your device, but this data is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not include third-party advertising or in-app purchases that track user behavior. We do not sell or trade user data to outside parties.
