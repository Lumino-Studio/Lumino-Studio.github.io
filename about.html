Our Story

Bridging Technology & Education

Based in Sydney, Australia, Lumino Studio was founded with a clear mission: to create digital experiences that make complex skills accessible. We believe that software should be more than just a tool—it should be an invitation to discover.

Our focus is on developing high-quality iOS and iPad applications that help children and lifelong learners master everything from the fundamentals of mathematics to the intricacies of music theory and language.

The Lumino Philosophy

Focus on Learning

We prioritize cognitive ease. Our apps are designed to minimize distraction and maximize engagement through clean, intuitive interfaces that respect the learner's journey.

Privacy First

In an era of data-tracking, we take a different path. We believe educational tools should be safe havens for exploration, which is why we collect no personal data from our users.

Our Expertise

With a deep technical foundation in Swift, Python, and Data Visualization, we combine engineering rigor with a confident, approachable aesthetic. Whether we are building an analog clock simulator or a Cantonese language module, every pixel is placed with purpose.
